Response Time & Availability

Everything you need to know about our service timing

Our average emergency response time is 15-30 minutes within our service areas. For urgent situations like lockouts, we prioritize immediate dispatch. Response times may vary depending on traffic conditions and your specific location, but we always strive to arrive as quickly as possible.

Yes! Our emergency locksmith services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays and weekends. Non-emergency services like scheduled installations and consultations are available during regular business hours (8 AM - 6 PM, Monday - Saturday).

Yes, there is an additional emergency service fee for calls outside regular business hours (8 PM - 6 AM) and holidays. The emergency fee is typically $60-80 depending on the service type and location. We always provide complete pricing information before starting any work.

Warranty & Guarantees

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction

We offer a comprehensive warranty on all our services and products:

  • Labor Warranty: 90 days on all installation and repair work
  • Hardware Warranty: 1-5 years depending on the product manufacturer
  • Emergency Services: 30-day satisfaction guarantee
  • Smart Locks: 1-year warranty on installation and configuration

All warranty terms are provided in writing before service begins.

Absolutely! We provide upfront pricing with no hidden fees. The price we quote is the price you pay. We also offer a price match guarantee - if you find a lower price from a licensed, insured competitor for the same service, we'll match it within 10%.

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, please contact us within 30 days and we'll make it right. This includes rework at no additional charge or a full refund if we're unable to resolve the issue to your satisfaction.

Payment Methods

Flexible payment options for your convenience

We accept a wide variety of payment methods for your convenience:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • Cash
  • Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)
  • Mobile payment apps (PayPal, Venmo, Zelle)
  • Business checks (for commercial accounts)

Payment is due upon completion of the service. Our technician will provide you with a detailed invoice and collect payment before leaving. For large commercial projects, we offer net 30 payment terms with approved credit applications.

For larger projects like complete security system installations, we offer flexible payment plans. These are available for projects over $1,000 and can be customized to fit your budget. Please ask our technician about payment plan options during your consultation.

Safety & Security

Your safety is our priority

Yes, absolutely. All our technicians undergo comprehensive background checks, including criminal history, driving records, and drug testing. We also verify their professional licenses and certifications. Your safety and security are our top priorities.

Yes, we are fully licensed by the state and carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation. We're also bonded for your protection. All our license and insurance information is available for review upon request, and we can provide copies before beginning any work.

We follow strict COVID-19 safety protocols:

  • Technicians wear masks and maintain social distancing
  • Regular hand sanitization and equipment disinfection
  • Contactless payment options available
  • Technicians stay home if feeling unwell
  • Virtual consultations available when appropriate

All our technicians arrive in clearly marked LockSmith Pro vehicles and wear company uniforms with photo ID badges. When they arrive, you can ask to see their company ID and state license. You can also call our office at 1-800-LOCKSMITH to verify the technician's identity and arrival.
