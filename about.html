About LockSmith Pro

Your trusted partner in professional security solutions with over 15 years of excellence in locksmith services

Our Story

Founded in 2009, LockSmith Pro began as a small family-owned business with a simple mission: provide reliable, professional locksmith services that people can trust.

Over the years, we've grown from a one-person operation to a team of 18+ certified technicians serving thousands of customers across the region. Our commitment to quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction has remained unchanged.

Today, we're proud to be the leading locksmith service provider, known for our rapid response times, expert technicians, and comprehensive security solutions for homes, businesses, and vehicles.

Safety & Compliance

Safety First Approach

At LockSmith Pro, safety is our top priority. We follow strict safety protocols on every job to protect our customers, their property, and our technicians.

  • Regular safety training and certification for all technicians
  • Proper protective equipment and tools for every job
  • COVID-19 safety protocols and sanitization procedures
  • Background-checked and drug-tested technicians

Regulatory Compliance

We maintain full compliance with all local, state, and federal regulations governing locksmith services.

  • State licensing requirements and renewals
  • Insurance and bonding compliance
  • Building code and safety standard adherence
  • Environmental and waste disposal regulations
